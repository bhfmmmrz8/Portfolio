About

I am a fun-loving Brooklyn based art-enthusiast with a focus in graphic design. I am a recent CCNY Art Dept / Electronic Design and Multimedia graduate. Some of my favorite things to do include painting, henna, calligraphy and bookbinding. I have recently completed a summer internship with Esther K. Smith at Purgatory Pie Press , where I assisted in various bookbinding projects. More recently I have been chosen to design the main visuals for Women's HerStory Month's upcoming event and conference at Borough of Manhattan COmmunity College, and will be hosting an exhibiton of my art in the gallery space at BMCC.

I previously acquired an Associate in Multimedia Arts and Communications from Borough of Manhattan Community College, and currently work as a Supplemental Instructor at the Learning Resource Center. My most enjoyable design aspects are hand drawn typography, vector illustration, logo/branding and publishing.

Visual Literature

Working from a sketch, the imagery resonates different aspects of daily life of every person metaphorically and directly.

The illustration talks about different notions of our lives and the things we are surrounded with. Colors, harmony, sweetness, happiness, sadness, challenges and the struggle we have to endure to get to the next step. All of these things mold us into the person we are today. Regardless of all the hardships, pain and sufferings which seem to overpower us are actually counterable and those too shall pass.

  • Software: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Service: Vector Illustration
