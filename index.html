Bookcover: A Different Flavor of Love

This is an illustration for the upcoming book od Author J.R. Floyd. The fruits are a play on the word flavor as it denotes the different relationships between the characters in the book.

  • Client: J. R. Floyd
  • Date: July 2017
  • Service: Illustration & layout Design

Visual Literature

Working from a sketch, the imagery resonates different aspects of daily life of every person metaphorically and directly.

The illustration talks about different notions of our lives and the things we are surrounded with. Colors, harmony, sweetness, happiness, sadness, challenges and the struggle we have to endure to get to next step. All of these things mold us into the person we are today. Regardless of all the hardships, pain and sufferings which seem to overpower us are actually counterable, and it too shall pass.

  • Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop
  • Date: November 2017
  • Service: Vector Illustration
